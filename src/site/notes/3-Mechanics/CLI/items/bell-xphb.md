# Bell
*Adventuring gear*  


- **Cost**: 1 gp

When rung as a [Utilize](3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/actions.md#Utilize) action, a Bell produces a sound that can be heard up to 60 feet away.

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 224. Available in the Free Rules (2024)*
